Welcome to Anjali’s Home Daycare!

About Me

I moved to Canada with my family in search of better opportunities and found my true calling in child care. With a background in customer service and a genuine love for children, I understand the importance of providing a reliable and trustworthy care option for parents.

Why Choose Anjali’s Home Daycare?

  • Experienced and Caring: With a passion for working with children, I provide a warm and friendly atmosphere where every child feels at home.
  • Safe and Secure: My home is fully child-proofed, ensuring a safe play and learning environment for your little ones.
  • Small Group Setting: By caring for a maximum of 5 children, I ensure that each child receives the individual attention they deserve.
  • Flexible and Affordable: I offer flexible hours and competitive rates to meet the needs of busy parents.
  • Engaging Activities: From creative arts and crafts to interactive playtime and educational activities, your child will enjoy a variety of engaging experiences.
